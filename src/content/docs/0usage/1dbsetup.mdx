Firstly, you need to create a database in your Notion to store and manage your posts.

<Steps>

1. Visit [this link](https://notion.so/dc5b49137cc0426a9b3ebfe4cc7f24a5?v=025e15f88d8847f987e370eb94346a9a) and click the "`Duplicate`" button on the top right corner:![image](./src/1dbstepup/step1.png)

2. Choose any workspace at your wish to save this template. ![image](./src/1dbstepup/step2.png)

3. Now you can see this database you just saved.![image](./src/1dbstepup/step3.png)

4. Copy the string from the URL shown in the red box, where located between `notion.so` and `?v=`. ![image](./src/1dbstepup/step4.png)

</Steps>
